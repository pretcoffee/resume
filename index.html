Peter Collins

Bio

Born in Brooklyn, NY, Peter Collins is a contemporary artist known for his innovative work in light and street art. His works have been exhibited in numerous galleries and festivals around the world.

Peter currently resides and works in San Francisco, CA, where he continues to explore new media and techniques in his art. His passion for creating visually striking pieces has earned him multiple awards and recognition in the art community.
